About Me

Hey! I’m Anthony Hernandez. I’m an animation major with a big passion for technology and creativity. I’ve always been into computers, and lately, I’ve been diving into web development to learn how to mix animation with cool, interactive web experiences. I’m all about creating accessible and inclusive tech, and I’m excited to use my skills to make a real impact. Thanks for stopping by my site and reach out if you’d like to connect!
